Wheel-Alignment Service

Car Wheel Alignment and Balancing

Wheels get damaged from a variety of causes including bad roads and potholes, leading to misalignment. Hence the wheels of your car need regular attention for you to ensure proper wheel alignment and balancing.

Importance of Wheel Alignment

It is of utmost importance that car owners get wheel alignment checks to prolong the life of their car’s tyres as much as possible. Misaligned tyres are much more likely to premature wear and tear. This in turn forces you to change your tyres in frequent intervals, adding unnecessary costs. You know that your car require wheel alignment if the car does not move in a straight line when you aren’t holding the steering wheel. If you notice this to be the case in your car, contact your nearest myTVS outlet immediately for wheel alignment done with the highest precision. Inappropriate alignment of the wheels can also lead to other problems in your car such as unusual vibrations while driving and problems with suspension.

Importance of wheel Balancing

myTVS technicians recommend tyre balancing as soon as you get new tyres for your car. This would ensure that all of the tyres have equal wheel weights. You’ll know if your car requires wheel balancing if your steering wheel is rocking back and forth as you drive. If you notice this in your car, you must immediately look for an MFC outlet near you as driving with wheels that require balancing can be very dangerous.

Benefits of wheel alignment

  • It will help reducing the maintenance budget of your car
  • It reduces the fuel consumption
  • It optimizes the lifetime of your tyres
  • It protects some mechanical parts of the vehicle
  • Your vehicle will emit less CO2
  • improve maneuverability and car control
