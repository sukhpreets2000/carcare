Windshield replacement

Everything You Need to Know About Car Windshield Maintenance!

Imagine a windshield with a chip or a crack. Not a pretty picture right? But the poor aesthetic appeal is no match to the safety hazard that a damaged windshield is. After all, contrary to what many believe, windshields for cars are anything but a mere decoration. Notwithstanding they render the car a complete and well-defined look, it’s their functional role that makes them the most vital of a car’s components.

Importance of Windshields for Cars

Windshields for cars are often seen as an ornamental addition by most car owners but that’s a wrong assumption. In a schema of car vital components, the windshield ranks very high. Your car windshield provides a clear vision of the road ahead. It protects you from outside elements such as wind, stones, dirt, water, etc. It also provides structural support to your car.

Replace Windshield Wipers Regularly

You might not know this but your windshield wipers also play a huge role in maintaining the health of windshield for cars. The wiper blades consist of a metal structure over which there is a rubber coating. Once the rubber coating is gone, your windshield glass is exposed to the metal structure. In such a condition, each time when you run the wipers, the metal scrapes against your windshield. This can leave serious scratches on your windshield glass. Moreover, daily wear-and-tear, debris, sunlight, and moisture can degrade the rubber coating on your windshield wipers. This can also subsequently damage your windshield glass. This is why it is recommended that you replace your wipers at least once a year to protect your windshield.
